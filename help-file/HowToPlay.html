How to Play


Once you've started a new game you'll be presented by a screen that looks like the one below:



The object of Hangman is to guess the mystery word (represented by asterisks) in the shortest amount of turns as possible. You can guess a letter by either clicking on the letter's button or by typing it with the keyboard. Each time you guess a letter wrong, your "hangman" will be hung a little more.



If you win, you'll be presented by a congratulations message and your score will be added to the database. Make sure that you have you speakers turned up high if you lose >:)
